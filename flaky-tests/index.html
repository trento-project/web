🔢 Math

Fliprate = Flips / Transitions

5 runs (P,F,P,F,P) = 4 flips / 4 trans = 1.0.

📈 Scores

  • 0.02-0.1: Mildly flaky
  • ~0.50: Highly unstable
  • 1.00: Theoretical max

🚫 Broken vs Flaky

Consistent failure = 0.0. Since it never "flips", it is Broken (a bug), not Flaky.

⚖️ Significance

Small sample sizes (e.g. 2 runs) can hit 1.0 easily. High repetition counts are needed for reliable data.
